Entrees

Penne a la Christi

Ingredients

Chicken Tenderloins 12
Olive oil 2 T
Diced garlic 2 T (more if you like garlic)
Sliced sun-dried tomatoes in oil 1/2 cup
Marinara sauce 2 jars
Heavy cream 2 cups
Grated parmesan cheese 1/2 c
Baby spinach (washed) 1 bag

Preparation

  • Cook chicken tenderloins until just cooked
  • Remove from pan and cut into bite sized pieces
  • Put 2 T olive oil into 8 qt sauce pan
  • Saute garlic and sundried tomatos
  • Add 2 cups heavy cream and cook on medium until just begins to bubble
  • Add 2 jars marinara sauce (I use Ragu or Bertolli)
  • Cook over medium heat until reduced stirring every couple minutes
  • Stir in 1/2 c grated parmesan cheese
  • Stir in spinach until wilted
  • Stir in cooked chicken
  • Cover and cook on low until pasta is ready
  • Cook 1 lb. Penne or small bowtie pasta
  • Drain pasta
  • Can be made without chicken if you prefer

Serve sauce over a bowl full of pasta. Great with italian bread for dipping in sauce.
Serves 6

Sides

Scrumptious Cheddar and Bacon Twice Baked Potatoes

Ingredients

Large pototoes 4
Bacon bits 3 oz. bag
Sharp shredded cheese 8oz. bag
Butter 1/2 cup

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  • Wash potatoes, dry, pierce on top with knife, wrap in paper towel
  • Microwave 5 mins, turn over
  • Microwave till done, they will be squishy (about 5-7 mins more depending on the size of the potato)
  • Cut a slice of the skin off the top of each potato
  • Hallow out potatoes placing potato pulp in large mixing bowl leaving the skins intact
  • Place skins on baking sheet
  • Mash the potatoes in the bowl with a fork
  • Mix in 1/2 cup softened butter and 1 cup cheese and stir till mixed well
  • Place potato mixture inside the skins
  • Top each potato with remaining cheese and bacon bits
  • Place in oven and bake for 10 mins

Desserts

Birthday Brownie Cake

Ingredients

Ghirardelli Double Chocolate Brownies 2 boxes
Eggs 2
Canola oil
Semi-sweet chocolate chips 1 cup
Pillsbury Creamy Supreme Chocolate Fudge Frosting 1 tub
Pam baking spray with flour The blue can (not cooking spray)
9x9 glass baking pans 2

Preparation

  • Preheat oven to temperature listed on box for glass pans
  • Spray bottom only of pans with Pam baking spray
  • Make brownies as directed on box with the addition of 1/2 choc. chips for each
  • Cook according to package directions for the minimum time listed
  • When brownies are cool at least 20 minutes - ice one pan of brownies with 1/2 of the icing
  • Loosen brownies in other pan and remove from pan and place on top of iced brownies
  • Ice brownie cake with the rest of the icing

Delicious served with chocolate or vanilla ice cream
